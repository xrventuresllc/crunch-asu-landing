Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑11‑07

We keep this page short and clear. This policy covers the pre‑launch website and waitlist for Crunch. If we change how we handle data, we’ll update this page and the date above.

What we collect

  • Email — required to notify you about Early Access and launch.
  • Optional preferences — goal, schedule (days/week & minutes), equipment, and whether you are a coach/trainer.
  • Engagement signals — simple counters that help us prioritize invites: Rep total (clicks), Plan Peek reveal, DM simulator runs, and referral attribution.
  • Technical basics — a random session ID (stored locally in your browser), your browser’s user agent, approximate timezone, referrer, and UTM parameters if present.

Note: Your IP address may appear in infrastructure logs handled by our providers for security and reliability. We don’t add IP to your waitlist record.

How we use data

  • Notify you about Early Access and launch.
  • Prioritize invite waves (e.g., Reps, referrals, coach/trainer flag).
  • Improve the product and measure interest (aggregate analytics).

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your data.
  • We don’t send spam. Expect 1–2 emails for waitlist/launch.
  • We don’t use invasive tracking cookies on this site.

Where your data lives (processors)

  • Supabase — stores the waitlist and event records (email, preferences, basic engagement signals).
  • Formspree — processes the form submission as a backup and for notifications.
  • Plausible — privacy‑friendly, cookieless analytics (aggregate traffic only).

We may add a security challenge (e.g., Cloudflare Turnstile) to curb abuse; if enabled, it may process minimal browser data for bot detection.

Local storage & cookies

  • We use localStorage to save your Rep count and a random session ID so you don’t lose progress between visits.
  • Plausible analytics is cookieless. We don’t set non‑essential cookies on this site.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime via the link in our emails.
  • Delete my data: email xrventuresllc@gmail.com from the address you used.
  • Update your preferences by replying to any waitlist email or emailing us directly.

Retention & security

  • We retain your waitlist info until you ask us to delete it or until it’s no longer needed.
  • Data is encrypted in transit; our providers also encrypt at rest.
  • Access is limited to the Crunch team for product and support purposes.

About Snap‑to‑Macro

Snap‑to‑Macro is in development and not active on this website. When released inside the app, it will be an optional feature that estimates daily macros from a photo. We’ll publish feature‑specific details before launch.

Questions? Contact xrventuresllc@gmail.com.
