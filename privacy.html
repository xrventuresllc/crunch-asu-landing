Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

This policy explains what information Lungeable collects, why we collect it, and how you can contact us. If you have questions, email support@lungeable.com.

Information we collect

  • Contact information (such as email) when you join the coach beta or request access.
  • Optional onboarding details you provide (coach focus, client count, notes).
  • Basic analytics such as page views and referral parameters (e.g., UTM tags) to understand which outreach works.
  • Technical metadata (such as user agent and referrer) to reduce spam and diagnose issues.

How we use information

  • To contact you about access, onboarding, product updates, and beta availability.
  • To improve the product and landing experience based on aggregated usage and feedback.
  • To prevent abuse (e.g., automated spam signups) and maintain service reliability.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use third-party providers for analytics, scheduling, or infrastructure. These providers process data only to deliver their services.

Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to operate the coach beta and provide access. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your choices

  • You can request removal from the coach beta list at any time.
  • You can request a copy of the information you submitted via the landing form.
